2.6 Length and Area

Length

Length is the distance between two points. It is a fundamental physical quantity, measured using various instruments depending on precision required.

SI Unit: meter (m)
Other Units: centimeter (cm), millimeter (mm), kilometer (km), inch (in), foot (ft), yard (yd), mile (mi)

Measuring Devices

  • Ruler: for lengths up to 30–50 cm, precision ±1 mm
  • Measuring tape (Tape rule): for longer distances, up to ~30 m; typical precision ±1 mm near the start, decreasing to ±1 cm at full length
  • Vernier caliper: for precise measurements up to 0.02 mm
  • Micrometer screw gauge: for extremely fine measurements, up to 0.01 mm

Example Conversions

  • 1 inch = 2.54 cm
  • 1 foot = 12 in = 0.3048 m
  • 1 mile = 5280 ft = 1.609 km
  • 1 km = 1000 m, 1 m = 100 cm, 1 cm = 10 mm

Conversion: Feet & Inches to Meters

Sometimes, measurements are given in imperial units (feet and inches). To convert to the SI unit meter (m), follow these steps:

  1. Recall the basic conversions:
    • 1 foot (ft) = 12 inches (in)
    • 1 inch = 0.0254 meters
    • 1 foot = 0.3048 meters
  2. Convert the feet portion to meters:

    Example: 5 ft → \( 5 \times 0.3048 = 1.524 \, \text{m} \)

  3. Convert the inches portion to meters:

    Example: 7 in → \( 7 \times 0.0254 = 0.1778 \, \text{m} \)

  4. Add the two results to get the total length in meters:

    \( 1.524 + 0.1778 = 1.7018 \, \text{m} \approx 1.70 \, \text{m} \)

Formula:
\[ \text{Length (m)} = \text{feet} \times 0.3048 + \text{inches} \times 0.0254 \]

Area

Area is the measure of the surface enclosed within a boundary. It is derived from length measurements.

SI Unit: square meter (m²)
Other Units: cm², mm², km², ft², in², acre, hectare

Measuring Area

  • Direct measurement for small surfaces using a ruler: Area = length × width
  • Planimeter: instrument to measure irregular surfaces
  • For large land areas: use maps, GPS, or satellite measurements

Example Conversions

  • 1 m² = 10,000 cm² = 1,000,000 mm²
  • 1 ft² = 0.092903 m²
  • 1 acre = 4046.86 m²
  • 1 hectare = 10,000 m²

Practice Problems

  1. Convert 5 km to meters, centimeters, and millimeters.
  2. Convert 3 ft 6 in to meters.
  3. Find the area in m² of a rectangle 25 cm × 40 cm.
  4. Convert 2 acres into m² and hectares.
  5. A field measures 120 m × 75 m. Find the area in m², cm², and km².
