Human Anatomy: Maintenance Quiz 1

Circulatory, Respiratory, Digestive & Excretory Systems

💪 Maintenance Systems Challenge

🔴 Circulatory Heart pumps blood
Arteries = away from heart
Veins = toward heart
Capillaries = exchange
🔵 Respiratory Alveoli = gas exchange
Diaphragm = breathing muscle
Epiglottis = blocks trachea
Hemoglobin = O₂ transport
🟠 Digestive Amylase = starch in mouth
Stomach = protein (pepsin)
Small intestine = absorption
Liver = bile production
🟢 Excretory Kidney = filtration
Nephron = functional unit
Bladder = urine storage
Liver = toxin filtration

This 20-question MCQ quiz tests your knowledge of the circulatory, respiratory, digestive, and excretory systems that maintain the body through nutrient transport, gas exchange, and waste removal.

🌿 Skills Tested:

  • ✓ Tracing blood flow through the heart and blood vessels
  • ✓ Identifying components of blood and their functions
  • ✓ Understanding gas exchange in the alveoli
  • ✓ Recognizing digestive enzymes and where they act
  • ✓ Describing kidney filtration and urine formation
  • ✓ Explaining the roles of liver, pancreas, and gallbladder
