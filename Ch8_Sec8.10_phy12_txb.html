Section 8.10: Chapter 8 Test

This test assesses understanding of all topics in Atomic and Nuclear Physics, including atomic structure, radioactivity, nuclear reactions, binding energy, and applications.

Chapter 8 Test Problems

  1. Calculate the binding energy of a nucleus given its mass defect of 0.015 u.
  2. Determine the half-life of a radioactive isotope with decay constant \( \lambda = 0.001 s^{-1} \).
  3. A nucleus undergoes alpha decay. Find the kinetic energy of the emitted alpha particle.
  4. Compute the number of undecayed nuclei after 10 half-lives.
  5. Compare energy released in fission of \( ^{235}U \) with fusion of \( ^2H + ^3H \).
  6. Determine the binding energy per nucleon for a nucleus with mass number 56.
  7. Sketch the energy level diagram for beta decay of a neutron-rich isotope.
  8. Solve a problem involving conservation of momentum in a nuclear reaction.
  9. Calculate total energy released in a given fusion reaction.
  10. Mixed conceptual and numerical questions covering all Chapter 8 topics.
