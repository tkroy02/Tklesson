Section 9.10: Chapter 9 Test

This is the comprehensive assessment for the Relativity unit. Test your understanding of Galilean relativity, special relativity, time dilation, length contraction, relativistic mass, energy-mass equivalence, and velocity addition.

Chapter 9 Test Problems

  1. A spacecraft moves at 0.75c relative to Earth. Calculate the time interval measured on Earth if 4 hours pass on the spacecraft.
  2. A rod of proper length 120 m moves at 0.85c. Determine its contracted length as observed from a stationary frame.
  3. Two spaceships move toward each other at 0.6c and 0.7c relative to Earth. Find their relative velocity.
  4. Calculate the relativistic kinetic energy of a 2 kg particle moving at 0.9c.
  5. Compute the total energy of a 1 kg particle moving at 0.95c.
  6. A muon with rest lifetime 2.2 Î¼s moves at 0.98c. Find its lifetime in the laboratory frame.
  7. A signal is sent from a spaceship moving at 0.8c. Use velocity addition to find its speed relative to Earth.
  8. A spacecraft travels at 0.7c for 5 hours as measured on Earth. Find proper time on the spacecraft.
  9. Calculate relativistic momentum of a 3 kg particle moving at 0.85c.
  10. A rod moving at 0.9c passes a stationary observer. Determine the contracted length and time interval for an event lasting 10 s in the rod's frame.
