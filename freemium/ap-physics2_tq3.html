🔵 DC Circuits Series: same current, voltage divides. Parallel: same voltage, current divides. Max power transfer when R load equals r internal.
🟠 Magnetic Fields & Forces Right-hand rule for B field around wire. F equals qv cross B for moving charges. Circular motion: r equals mv over qB.
🟢 Electromagnetic Induction Faraday's law: EMF equals negative dPhi over dt. Lenz's law: induced current opposes flux change. EMF equals A times dB over dt.
🔴 Circuit Analysis Terminal voltage rises as external R increases. Bulb brightness depends on power. Dielectric increases C, Q, and U at constant V.
