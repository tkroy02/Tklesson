🔵 Geometric Optics Thin lens equation: 1 over f equals 1 over do plus 1 over di. Mirror equation same form. Magnification: m equals negative di over do.
🟠 Wave Optics Double-slit: delta y equals lambda L over d. Thin film interference depends on phase shifts at boundaries. Wavelength shortens in medium.
🟢 Quantum Physics Photoelectric: Kmax equals hf minus phi. Bohr: En equals negative 13.6 eV over n squared. De Broglie: lambda equals h over p equals h over mv.
🔴 Polarization Malus's law: I equals I0 cos squared theta. Unpolarized through first polarizer: intensity halved. TIR: sine theta c equals n2 over n1.
