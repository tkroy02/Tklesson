🔵 Rigid Transformations Translations, reflections, and rotations preserve congruence. Coordinate rules map pre-images to images precisely.
🟠 Dilations & Scale Dilations multiply distances by scale factor k. Perimeter scales linearly. Area scales by k squared.
🟢 Coordinate Geometry Distance formula: square root of delta x squared plus delta y squared. Midpoint: average of x and y coordinates. Slope: rise over run.
🔴 Circles & Perpendicular Lines Circle equation: (x minus h) squared plus (y minus k) squared equals r squared. Perpendicular lines have negative reciprocal slopes.
