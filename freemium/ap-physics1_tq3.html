🔵 Work & Energy W equals Fd cos phi. Work-energy theorem: W net equals delta K. Conservative forces: path independent, PE defined.
🟠 Conservation of Energy E initial equals E final. Pendulum: mgh becomes one-half mv squared. Loop-the-loop: minimum height is 5R over 2.
🟢 Springs U s equals one-half kx squared. Compression doubles, energy quadruples. Max compression proportional to initial speed.
🔴 Power P equals W over t equals Fv. Constant power: v equals square root of 2Pt over m. Constant speed lift: P equals mgv.
