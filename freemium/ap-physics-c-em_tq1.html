🔵 Gauss's Law Flux equals q enc over epsilon0. Choose Gaussian surfaces by symmetry. Line: E proportional to 1 over r. Sphere: E proportional to r inside.
🟠 Potential & Gradient E equals negative gradient V. Delta V equals negative integral E dot dr. Equipotential surfaces perpendicular to field lines.
🟢 Capacitance C equals Q over V. Spherical: C equals 4 pi epsilon0 ab over b minus a. Parallel plates: C equals epsilon0 A over d.
🔴 Work & Energy U equals kq1q2 over r for point charges. Work to assemble equals total potential energy. Method of images for planes.
