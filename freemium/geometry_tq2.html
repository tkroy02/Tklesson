🔵 Similarity Corresponding angles congruent. Side lengths proportional. Area scales by square of linear scale factor.
🟠 Right Triangle Trig SOHCAHTOA. Sine equals opposite over hypotenuse. Cosine equals adjacent over hypotenuse. Tangent equals opposite over adjacent.
🟢 Applications Shadow problems use similar triangles. Angle of elevation and depression. Ladder-wall and cliff-height scenarios.
🔴 Key Relationships Cofunction identity: sin(A) equals cos(90 minus A). Triangle Proportionality Theorem. Similar polygons preserve angle measures.
