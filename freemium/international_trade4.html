🔵 Current Account Records trade in goods, services, primary income, and secondary income. Deficit matched by financial account surplus.
🟠 Financial Account Records capital inflows/outflows. Foreign purchase of domestic assets = credit (capital inflow).
🟢 Purchasing Power Parity Exchange rates adjust to equalize the price of identical goods across countries. Inflation differentials drive currency movements.
🔴 Interest Parity Conditions Covered (CIP) uses forward contracts; Uncovered (UIP) relies on expected exchange rate changes to equalize returns.
