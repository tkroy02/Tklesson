🔵 Linear Kinematics v equals v0 plus at. x equals x0 plus v0t plus one-half at squared. v squared equals v0 squared plus 2a delta x.
🟠 Projectile Motion Horizontal: constant velocity, ax equals zero. Vertical: constant acceleration, ay equals negative g. Flight time independent of horizontal motion.
🟢 Interpreting Graphs Position-time: slope equals velocity, curvature equals acceleration. Velocity-time: slope equals acceleration, area equals displacement.
🔴 Multi-Phase Analysis Break motion into segments. Use final conditions of one phase as initial conditions for the next. Catching-up: set positions equal.
