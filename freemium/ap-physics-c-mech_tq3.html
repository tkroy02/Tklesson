🔵 Line Integrals & Work W equals integral of F dot dr along path. Parameterize path, substitute, integrate. Work depends on path unless force is conservative.
🟠 Conservative Forces Curl test: dFx over dy equals dFy over dx. Potential energy: F equals negative gradient U. U found by integrating force components.
🟢 Collisions Elastic: momentum and kinetic energy conserved. Inelastic: momentum conserved, objects stick. Impulse equals integral of F dt equals delta p.
🔴 Energy Applications Spring energy: one-half kx squared. Power: P equals Fv. Ballistic pendulum: two-stage (momentum then energy). Center of mass velocity.

This 10-question MCQ quiz tests your understanding of line integrals for work, conservative force criteria, elastic and inelastic collisions, impulse-momentum theorem, and energy conservation with calculus at the AP Physics C level.
