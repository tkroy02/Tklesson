🔵 Perfect Competition Price takers. P equals MC (allocative). P equals min ATC (productive). Long-run zero economic profit. Shutdown if P is less than AVC.
🟠 Monopoly Single seller. MR below demand. Produces where MR equals MC. Creates deadweight loss (P greater than MC). Price discrimination recovers efficiency.
🟢 Monopolistic Competition Many firms, differentiated products. Low barriers. Long-run: P equals ATC but excess capacity. Advertising and brand loyalty.
🔴 Oligopoly Few interdependent firms. Game theory: dominant strategies, Nash equilibrium. Kinked demand curve explains price rigidity.
