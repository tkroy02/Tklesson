🔵 Circle Geometry Sector area: fraction of circle area. Arc length: fraction of circumference. Radius perpendicular to chord bisects it.
🟠 3D Solids Cylinder: V equals pi r squared h. Cone: V equals one-third pi r squared h. Sphere: SA equals 4 pi r squared.
🟢 Power Theorems Tangent perpendicular to radius. Secant-Secant: external times whole equals external times whole.
🔴 Surface Area Cone SA: pi r squared plus pi r l. Rectangular prism SA: 2(lw plus lh plus wh). Sphere SA: 4 pi r squared.
