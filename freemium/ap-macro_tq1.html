🔵 PPC & Trade Asymmetric productivity growth pivots the curve. Comparative advantage: lowest opportunity cost drives specialization.
🟠 GDP & Price Levels Nominal GDP − Real GDP = inflation/deflation. CPI tracks cost of living. Unexpected inflation redistributes wealth.
🟢 Unemployment Types Frictional (seasonal/search), structural (skill mismatch), cyclical (demand-deficient). Natural rate excludes cyclical.
🔴 Business Cycles Peak → contraction: GDP slows, inventories accumulate, cyclical unemployment rises. Supply shocks shift SRAS.
