🔵 Faraday's Law EMF equals negative dPhi over dt. Sliding rod: EMF equals BLv. Rotating loop: EMF equals NBA omega sin(omega t).
🟠 Inductance Self: L equals mu0 N squared A over ell. Mutual: M equals mu0 N1 N2 A over ell. LR time constant: tau equals L over R.
🟢 Maxwell's Equations Displacement current: Id equals epsilon0 dPhi E over dt. Induced E field: closed integral E dot dl equals negative dPhi B over dt.
🔴 Energy & Oscillations U equals one-half L I squared. Magnetic energy density: u equals B squared over 2 mu0. LC: omega equals 1 over sqrt(LC).
