🔵 Factor Markets MRP equals MP times Price. Hire where MRP equals Wage. Monopsony: MFC above labor supply, hires less, pays less.
🟠 Externalities Negative: MSC greater than MPC, overproduction. Positive: MSB greater than MPB, underproduction. Pigouvian taxes and subsidies restore efficiency.
🟢 Public Goods Non-rival and non-excludable. Free-rider problem causes underprovision. Government provision via taxation is the standard solution.
🔴 Information & Behavior Adverse selection: pre-transaction (lemons). Moral hazard: post-transaction (risky behavior). Coase Theorem: low transaction costs plus clear property rights.

This 10-question MCQ quiz tests your understanding of factor market hiring decisions, externalities, public goods, asymmetric information, and the Coase Theorem.
