🔵 Ricardian Model Comparative advantage via labor productivity differences. Both nations gain when terms of trade fall between domestic opportunity costs.
🟠 Heckscher-Ohlin & Theorems Factor endowments drive trade. Stolper-Samuelson: price ↑ → intensive factor gains. Rybczynski: endowment ↑ → asymmetric output shift.
🟢 New Trade Theory Monopolistic competition + economies of scale explain intra-industry trade. Consumers gain variety alongside lower prices.
🔴 Gravity & Linder Trade ∝ GDP₁×GDP₂ / distance. Linder: similar income → similar preferences → more trade in differentiated goods.
