🔵 Rotational Dynamics tau equals I alpha equals I times d omega over dt. Rolling: v equals omega R, a equals alpha R. Variable torque: integrate to find omega of t.
🟠 Rolling & Slipping Friction provides torque. Transition from slipping to pure rolling when v equals omega R. Solve coupled translation-rotation equations.
🟢 Oscillations Physical pendulum: omega equals sqrt(Mgd over I). Damped: m times x double dot plus b times x dot plus kx equals zero. omega d equals sqrt(omega0 squared minus gamma squared).
🔴 Angular Momentum L equals r cross p equals m times r cross v. Conserved without external torque. Variable I: Ii times omegai equals If times omegaf. Work done equals delta K.
