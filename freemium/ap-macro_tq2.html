🔵 Aggregate Demand Downward sloping: wealth effect, interest-rate effect, net export effect. Shifts from C, I, G, or NX changes.
🟠 Short-Run & Long-Run AS SRAS: upward sloping (sticky wages). LRAS: vertical at full employment. Supply shocks shift both curves.
🟢 Output Gaps Recessionary gap: SR equilibrium below LRAS. Inflationary gap: SR equilibrium above LRAS. Self-correction via wage flexibility.
🔴 Fiscal Multipliers Spending multiplier = 1/(1−MPC). Tax multiplier = −MPC/(1−MPC). Autonomous spending shifts AD by multiplier effect.
