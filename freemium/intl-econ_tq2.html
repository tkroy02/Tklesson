🔵 Welfare Analysis Tariff removal → net welfare gain. Quota vs. tariff: rent capture determines welfare difference. Export subsidy = worst policy.
🟠 Strategic Trade Policy Brander-Spencer: subsidy shifts oligopoly profits. Critique: retaliation risk + information requirements.
🟢 Political Economy Concentrated producers lobby; diffuse consumers free-ride. Grossman-Helpman: protection sold for contributions.
🔴 Optimum Tariff Large country: ToT gain > deadweight loss → net welfare increase. Production subsidy avoids consumption distortion.
