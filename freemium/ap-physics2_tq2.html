🔵 Coulomb's Law F equals kq1q2 over r squared. Conductors share charge equally on contact. Force proportional to product of charges.
🟠 Electric Field E equals F over q equals kQ over r squared. Field lines from positive to negative. Zero field where vectors cancel.
🟢 Electric Potential V equals kQ over r. E equals negative delta V over delta x. Potential constant inside conductors. Energy: U equals kq1q2 over r.
🔴 Gauss's Law Field inside hollow conductor is zero. Charge resides on outer surface. Parallel plates: E equals delta V over d.
