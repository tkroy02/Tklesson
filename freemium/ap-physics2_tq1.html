🔵 Fluid Dynamics Continuity: A1v1 equals A2v2. Bernoulli: P plus one-half rho v squared plus rho gy is constant. Venturi effect.
🟠 Buoyancy Archimedes: buoyant force equals weight of displaced fluid. Floating: displaced weight equals object weight. Ice melts without overflow.
🟢 Thermodynamics First law: delta U equals Q plus W. Isothermal: delta U equals zero. Carnot efficiency: 1 minus Tc over Th. PV work equals area under curve.
🔴 Kinetic Theory Temperature proportional to average translational KE. vrms proportional to square root of T. Doubling vrms quadruples temperature.
