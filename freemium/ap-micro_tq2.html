🔵 Utility Maximization MU/P equalization rule. When MU per dollar differs, shift consumption toward the good with higher MU/P.
🟠 Production Costs MP equals change in Q divided by change in L. MRP equals MP times Price. MC equals change in TC divided by change in Q.
🟢 Returns to Scale Increasing returns: output grows faster than inputs. LRATC is envelope of SRATC curves. MC above ATC pulls ATC up.
🔴 Budget Constraints Price change pivots budget line. Diminishing marginal returns explains rising short-run MC. LR diseconomies from management bottlenecks.

This 10-question MCQ quiz tests your understanding of consumer utility maximization, marginal product and revenue product, cost curve relationships, and returns to scale.
