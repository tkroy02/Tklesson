🔵 Fiscal Policy Spending multiplier = 1/(1−MPC). Tax multiplier = −MPC/(1−MPC). Combined policies shift AD.
🟠 Monetary Policy Open-market operations, discount rate, IORB. Money multiplier = 1/rr. QE at zero lower bound.
🟢 Banking System Excess reserves × multiplier = new money. IORB rate sets floor on short-term rates.
🔴 Policy Lags Fiscal: long inside lag. Monetary: short inside lag, long outside lag. Crowding out offsets fiscal stimulus.
