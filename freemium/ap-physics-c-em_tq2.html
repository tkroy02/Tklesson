🔵 Capacitor Networks Series: reciprocal sum. Parallel: direct sum. Bridge networks: use symmetry to identify equipotential points.
🟠 Dielectrics C equals kappa times C0. Battery connected: V constant, Q and U increase. Battery disconnected: Q constant, V decreases.
🟢 Energy & Forces U equals one-half CV squared equals Q squared over 2C. Energy density: u equals one-half kappa epsilon0 E squared. Dielectric pulled into capacitor.
🔴 Cylindrical & Spherical Coaxial: C equals 2 pi epsilon0 L over ln(b over a). Spherical: C equals 4 pi epsilon0 ab over (b minus a). Derive via Gauss's law.
