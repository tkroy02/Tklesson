🔵 Ricardian Model Comparative advantage based on labor productivity differences. Opportunity cost determines trade patterns.
🟠 Heckscher-Ohlin Theorem Factor endowments drive trade. Export goods intensive in abundant factors; import goods intensive in scarce factors.
🟢 New Trade Theory Economies of scale & monopolistic competition explain intra-industry trade between similar countries.
🔴 Gravity Model Trade volume ∝ GDP₁ × GDP₂ / distance. Larger economies trade more; greater distance reduces trade.
