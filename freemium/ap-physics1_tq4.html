🔵 Momentum Conservation p equals mv. Total momentum conserved when no external forces act. Vector quantity with direction.
🟠 Impulse J equals F avg times delta t equals delta p. Area under F-t graph equals impulse. Equals change in momentum.
🟢 Elastic Collisions Both momentum and kinetic energy conserved. Relative speed unchanged. Equal masses swap velocities.
🔴 Inelastic Collisions Momentum conserved, kinetic energy lost. Perfectly inelastic: objects stick together. Ballistic pendulum combines collision and swing.
