🔵 RC Circuits Charging: q equals C E times (1 minus e to the negative t over RC). Discharging: q equals Q0 e to the negative t over RC. Time constant tau equals RC.
🟠 Kirchhoff's Rules Junction rule: sum of currents in equals sum out. Loop rule: sum of potential changes around closed loop equals zero.
🟢 Biot-Savart Law dB equals mu0 over 4pi times I dl cross r-hat over r squared. Circular loop axis: B equals mu0 I R squared over 2(R squared plus x squared) to the 3/2.
🔴 Ampère's Law Closed integral of B dot dl equals mu0 I enc. Solenoid: B equals mu0 n I. Thick wire inside: B proportional to r. Force between wires: mu0 I1 I2 over 2pi d.
