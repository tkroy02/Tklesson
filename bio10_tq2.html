Biochemistry: Macromolecules & Polymers — The Four Biomolecules

🧬 Macromolecules Challenge

🍞 Carbs Monomer: monosaccharide
Bond: glycosidic
Ratio: C:H:O = 1:2:1
Starch, glycogen, cellulose
🧈 Lipids Monomer: fatty acids + glycerol
Bond: ester
Hydrophobic
Triglycerides, phospholipids, steroids
💪 Proteins Monomer: amino acids
Bond: peptide
4 levels of structure
Enzymes, structure, transport
🧬 Nucleic Acids Monomer: nucleotide
Bond: phosphodiester
DNA stores genetic info
RNA folds into 3D shapes
🔴 Dehydration Synthesis Builds polymers
Removes H₂O
One monomer loses –OH, other loses –H
Forms covalent bond
🔵 Hydrolysis Breaks polymers
Adds H₂O
–OH attaches to one monomer
–H attaches to the other

  • ✓ Understanding protein structure levels
  • ✓ Comparing DNA and RNA structure and function
