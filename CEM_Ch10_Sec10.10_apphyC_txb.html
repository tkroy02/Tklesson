Section 10.10: Mock Exam

This mock exam tests comprehensive understanding of Chapters 7–10, covering magnetic fields, EM induction, AC/DC circuits, electromagnetic waves, and Maxwell’s equations.

Mock Exam Questions

  1. A particle with charge q and mass m enters a uniform magnetic field. Determine its trajectory and period.
  2. Derive the expression for induced EMF in a coil rotating in a uniform magnetic field.
  3. Compute current and voltage in an RLC series circuit at resonance and determine the quality factor Q.
  4. Calculate the energy density and Poynting vector for a plane EM wave with given E and B fields.
  5. Apply boundary conditions to a wave incident on a dielectric interface and find reflected/transmitted amplitudes.
  6. Verify Maxwell’s equations for a plane EM wave propagating in free space.
  7. Compute the electric potential energy of a system of multiple point charges.
  8. Find the torque on a rectangular current loop in a uniform magnetic field and its stable orientation.
  9. Given a time-varying magnetic flux, calculate the induced EMF and current in a loop with known resistance.
  10. Solve AP-style multi-step problem combining EM waves, circuits, and Maxwell’s equations.
