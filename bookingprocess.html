Booking Process

Simple steps to schedule your tutoring sessions and start learning

How to Book Sessions

Follow these simple steps to schedule your tutoring sessions

1

Create Your Student Account

Start by signing up for a student account on our platform. Provide your basic information and academic needs.

  • Quick registration process
  • Secure account creation
  • Academic level and subject selection
2

Get Matched with a Tutor

Our system automatically matches you with qualified tutors based on your subject needs and preferences.

  • Automatic tutor matching
  • Review tutor profiles and ratings
  • Based on subject expertise and availability
3

Purchase Session Credits

Before booking sessions, purchase credits or packages through your student dashboard.

  • Navigate to "Packages" section
  • Choose from individual credits or package deals
  • Secure payment processing
  • Credits are required to book sessions
4

Communicate with Your Tutor

Use the built-in chat system to discuss availability, session goals, and scheduling preferences.

  • Direct messaging with your assigned tutor
  • Discuss learning objectives
  • Coordinate optimal session times
  • Plan session content in advance
5

Book Your Session

Once you and your tutor agree on timing, book the session directly through your student dashboard.

  • Select date and time
  • Choose session duration (standard 60 minutes)
  • Session is automatically added to both calendars
  • Receive confirmation email with details
6

Join Your Session

Access your session through the student dashboard when it's time to learn.

  • "Join Session" button appears 15 minutes before start time
  • Automatic redirect to virtual meeting room
  • Interactive online whiteboard and screen sharing
  • Post-session rating and review system
