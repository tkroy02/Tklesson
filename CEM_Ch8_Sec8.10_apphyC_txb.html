Section 8.10: Practice Problems

  1. Compute the magnetic field energy for a solenoid of given dimensions and current.
  2. Calculate the total energy stored in a toroid with a specified current and number of turns.
  3. Determine the energy density at a point where two magnetic fields superpose.
  4. Compare energy stored in two solenoids of equal length but different radii.
  5. Demonstrate how energy in an RL circuit is dissipated when current decreases.
