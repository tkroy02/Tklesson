Section 10.9: AP-Style Review Problems

Practice with AP-style conceptual and calculation problems to solidify understanding of all chapters.

Review Problems

  1. Calculate the electric field at a point 0.5 m from a 2 μC point charge.
  2. A parallel-plate capacitor has plates separated by 1 mm with charge 5 μC. Determine the electric field between the plates.
  3. Determine the magnetic field at the center of a circular loop of radius 0.1 m carrying 3 A of current.
  4. A 10 Ω resistor and a 2 H inductor are in series with a 12 V battery. Determine the current after 0.5 s.
  5. Explain qualitatively the propagation of an electromagnetic wave in free space.
  6. Using Gauss’s Law, find the electric flux through a spherical surface enclosing a charge of 4 μC.
  7. Discuss boundary conditions for electric and magnetic fields at the interface between two media.
  8. Derive the expression for the RMS voltage of a sinusoidal AC source.
