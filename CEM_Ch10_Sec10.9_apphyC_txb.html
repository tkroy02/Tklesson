Section 10.9: AP-Style Review Problems

This section provides AP-level review problems covering all key topics in Chapters 7–10 of the course.

AP-Style Problems

  1. A charge moves through a uniform magnetic field. Determine the force, trajectory, and period of motion.
  2. Compute the energy stored in a capacitor and an inductor in an LC circuit and show energy conservation.
  3. Derive the speed of an electromagnetic wave in a medium with given permittivity and permeability.
  4. Calculate the Poynting vector and average intensity of an EM wave with specified E and B fields.
  5. Apply boundary conditions to find reflected and transmitted wave amplitudes at a dielectric interface.
  6. Solve for current and voltage in an RLC series circuit at resonance.
  7. Analyze Faraday’s law and induced EMF in a rotating loop within a magnetic field.
  8. Use Maxwell’s equations to show that EM waves are transverse and satisfy the wave equation in free space.
  9. Given a system of point charges, compute the electric potential energy and forces.
  10. Evaluate the torque on a current loop in a uniform magnetic field and determine its equilibrium orientation.
