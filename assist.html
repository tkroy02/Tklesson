FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know about our tutoring services

Can I use my Self-Learning account to log in?
No. The Student-Tutoring portal is completely separate from the Self-Learning portal. You need to create a new account specifically for tutoring services.
How do I sign up for tutoring?
Click the "Create Account" button above, fill out the registration form, and you'll be able to browse and book tutors immediately.
What subjects are available?
We offer tutoring in 40+ subjects including Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Economics, English, and many more across all grade levels.
How much does tutoring cost?

We offer flexible pricing to fit your needs:

  • Single Session $50 (+ tax)
  • Bronze Package (4 sessions) $185
  • Silver Package (8 sessions) $365
  • Gold Package (12 sessions) $524

Visit our Tutoring Rates page for full details.

Can I switch tutors if I'm not satisfied?
Absolutely. You can book sessions with different tutors until you find the right fit. There's no long-term commitment required.
