1. \( \lim_{x \to 2} \frac{x^2 - 4}{x - 2} \)

Step: Factor numerator: \( \frac{(x-2)(x+2)}{x-2} = x+2 \). Evaluate: \( 2+2 = 4 \)

2. \( \lim_{x \to 1} \frac{x^3 - 1}{x - 1} \)

Step: Factor: \( \frac{(x-1)(x^2+x+1)}{x-1} = x^2+x+1 \). Evaluate: \( 1+1+1 = 3 \)

3. \( \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{x^2 + 3x}{x} \)

Step: Split fraction: \( x + 3 \). Evaluate: \( 0 + 3 = 3 \)

4. \( \lim_{x \to 5} \frac{x^2 - 25}{x - 5} \)

Step: Factor numerator: \( (x-5)(x+5)/(x-5) = x+5 \). Evaluate: \( 5+5=10 \)

5. \( \lim_{x \to 3} (2x + 1) = 2(3)+1 = 7 \)

6. \( \lim_{x \to -1} (x^2 + 4x + 3) = (-1)^2 + 4(-1) + 3 = 0 \)

7. \( \lim_{x \to 4} \frac{x^2 - 16}{x - 4} \)

Step: Factor numerator: \( (x-4)(x+4)/(x-4) = x+4 \). Evaluate: \( 4+4=8 \)

8. \( \lim_{x \to 0} (5x+2) = 2 \)

9. \( \lim_{x \to 2} \frac{x^3 - 8}{x - 2} \)

Step: Factor numerator: \( (x-2)(x^2+2x+4)/(x-2) = x^2+2x+4 \). Evaluate: \( 4+4+4=12 \)

10. \( \lim_{x \to -2} \frac{x^2 + 2x}{x+2} \)

Step: Factor: \( x(x+2)/(x+2) = x \). Evaluate: \( -2 \)

11. \( \lim_{x \to 1} \frac{x^2 - 1}{x - 1} = 2 \)

12. \( \lim_{x \to 3} \frac{x^2 - 9}{x - 3} = 6 \)

13. \( \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{x^3 + 2x^2}{x} \)

Step: Split: \( x^2 + 2x \). Evaluate: 0

14. \( \lim_{x \to 2} (3x+1) = 7 \)

15. \( \lim_{x \to -1} (x^2 + 2x) = -1 \)

16. \( \lim_{x \to 5} \frac{x^2 - 25}{x-5} = 10 \)

17. \( \lim_{x \to 4} \frac{x^2 - 16}{x-4} = 8 \)

18. \( \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{x^2 - 4x}{x} \)

Step: Split: \( x - 4 \). Evaluate: -4

19. \( \lim_{x \to 2} \frac{4x^2 - 9}{2x - 3} \)

Step: Evaluate directly: \( (4*4-9)/(4-3)=7 \)
