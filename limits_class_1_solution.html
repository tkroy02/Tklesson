1. \( \lim_{x \to \infty} \frac{2x+1}{x} \)

Step: Divide numerator and denominator by \(x\): \( \frac{2 + 1/x}{1} = 2 \)

2. \( \lim_{x \to \infty} \frac{3x^2+5}{x^2-1} \)

Step: Divide numerator and denominator by \(x^2\): \( \frac{3 + 5/x^2}{1 - 1/x^2} = 3 \)

3. \( \lim_{x \to \infty} \frac{5x^3-2x}{2x^3+7} \)

Step: Divide numerator and denominator by \(x^3\): \( \frac{5 - 2/x^2}{2 + 7/x^3} = 5/2 \)

4. \( \lim_{x \to \infty} \frac{x^2+4x+1}{2x^2-3} \)

Step: Divide numerator and denominator by \(x^2\): \( \frac{1 + 4/x + 1/x^2}{2 - 3/x^2} = 1/2 \)

5. \( \lim_{x \to \infty} \frac{7x^4+3}{x^4+5} \)

Step: Divide numerator and denominator by \(x^4\): \( \frac{7 + 3/x^4}{1 + 5/x^4} = 7 \)

6. \( \lim_{x \to \infty} \frac{x^3-4x+1}{x^3+2} \)

Step: Divide numerator and denominator by \(x^3\): \( \frac{1 - 4/x^2 + 1/x^3}{1 + 2/x^3} = 1 \)

7. \( \lim_{x \to \infty} \frac{6x^2-5}{3x^2+1} \)

Step: Divide numerator and denominator by \(x^2\): \( \frac{6 - 5/x^2}{3 + 1/x^2} = 2 \)

8. \( \lim_{x \to \infty} \frac{x^5+2x}{2x^5-3} \)

Step: Divide numerator and denominator by \(x^5\): \( \frac{1 + 2/x^4}{2 - 3/x^5} = 1/2 \)

9. \( \lim_{x \to \infty} \frac{4x^3+7x}{x^3-1} \)

Step: Divide numerator and denominator by \(x^3\): \( \frac{4 + 7/x^2}{1 - 1/x^3} = 4 \)

10. \( \lim_{x \to \infty} \frac{9x^2+1}{3x^2+5} \)

Step: Divide numerator and denominator by \(x^2\): \( \frac{9 + 1/x^2}{3 + 5/x^2} = 3 \)
