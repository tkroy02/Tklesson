Bioenergetics: Thermodynamics & Gibbs Free Energy

⚡ Bioenergetics Challenge

Gibbs Free Energy Equation
ΔG = ΔH − TΔS
ΔG < 0 = Exergonic (spontaneous)  |  ΔG > 0 = Endergonic (non-spontaneous)
🔵 1st Law Energy cannot be created or destroyed
Total energy of universe = constant
Energy transforms between forms
🔴 2nd Law Entropy of universe always increases
Cells maintain order by releasing heat
Equilibrium = maximum entropy
🟠 ΔG Sign Rules –ΔH + –ΔS → spontaneous at low T
+ΔH + +ΔS → spontaneous at high T
+ΔH + –ΔS → never spontaneous
🟢 ATP Hydrolysis ATP → ADP + Pᵢ
ΔG ≈ –30.5 kJ/mol
Strongly exergonic
Drives endergonic reactions
