Request to Delete Your Data

We respect your privacy and your right to control your personal information.

You can request deletion of your data from our platform at any time.

Data We Store

  • Personal Information: Name, email, phone number, age
  • Academic Information: School name, grade level, subjects
  • Parent/Guardian Details: Names and contact information
  • Platform Data: Session history, chat messages, booking records
  • Account Information: User preferences, tutor matches, payment history

What Happens When You Request Deletion

  • All your personal data is permanently deleted from our systems
  • Your account and all associated records are removed
  • Session history and chat messages are erased
  • You will no longer have access to the platform
Note: We process deletion requests within 72 hours. Once deleted, data cannot be recovered. If you wish to use our services again, you'll need to create a new account.

How to Request Data Deletion

Email us at info@tklesson.com with the subject line: "Data Deletion Request".

Please include:

  • The email address associated with your Tklesson account
  • Your full name
  • Confirmation that you want all data permanently deleted

We will confirm your identity and complete the deletion within 72 hours.

For any questions about data privacy, contact us at info@tklesson.com.
