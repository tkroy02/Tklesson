Section 10.2: Measures of Central Tendency

This section covers mean, median, mode, and weighted averages to describe the center of data distributions.

Example 1: Mean

Find the mean of the data: 4, 5, 7, 10, 4.

Step 1: Sum the data: 4+5+7+10+4 = 30

Step 2: Divide by number of values: 30 รท 5 = 6

Mean = 6

Example 2: Median & Mode

Find the median and mode of: 3, 5, 3, 8, 7.

Step 1: Arrange data in ascending order: 3, 3, 5, 7, 8

Median = middle value = 5

Mode = most frequent value = 3
