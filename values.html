Our Values

The principles that guide everything we build and every decision we make

People Over Profit

Tklesson is not a profit-first company. We are a platform built to serve students, families, and educators — not to extract value from them. Every feature we build, every policy we write, starts with a simple question: does this help the people who use our platform?

We are self-funded and independent. We have no investors demanding growth at the expense of our users. We answer only to the students, parents, and tutors who trust us with their education.

Fair, Transparent Pricing

Students pay $50 USD per one-on-one tutoring session. Of that, $40 USD goes directly to the tutor. The remaining $10 USD covers platform operations — hosting, security, support, and continuous improvement.

There are no hidden fees, no surprise charges, and no confusing terms. Our package pricing is public, our refund policy is clear, and every client knows exactly where their money goes.

$50 session → $40 to tutor · $10 to platform

Quality Over Quantity, Always

We would rather have a small team of exceptional tutors than a large roster of unreliable ones. Every tutor on Tklesson is carefully vetted for subject mastery, teaching ability, and character. We recruit dedicated, competent, and genuinely caring educators — because the person standing in front of a student matters more than the number of tutors on our platform.

Our tutors are not competing in a race to the bottom. They are building meaningful relationships with students, and we make sure their compensation reflects the value they bring — not just to our platform, but to the lives they impact.

Built for Schools, Not Just Individuals

We serve schools with the same respect and transparency we offer every student and tutor. Our school platform provides teachers with powerful tools — gradebooks, assignment tracking, quiz monitoring, and enrolment management — without replacing their expertise with automation.

School pricing is tiered by student count so small schools are never priced out. There are no hidden enterprise fees, no long-term lock-in contracts, and no penalties for leaving. Schools can export their data at any time and retain full control over their educational records.

We are FERPA-aware and committed to student privacy. School administrators decide who accesses their data, and we provide the tools to enforce those decisions — including audit logs, data exports, and account deletion upon request.

Your Data Is Yours

We do not sell, share, or monetize user data. Student records, session history, and personal information are used only for the educational functions they serve — nothing else. We have no partnerships, no affiliates, and no data-sharing agreements with any third party.

If you request deletion of your data, we comply fully and promptly. Your information belongs to you, not to us. Our platform is protected by the highest security protocols to ensure your data stays private and secure at all times.

Education Without Barriers

Our goal is to make quality education available to everyone — regardless of location, background, or income. From K-12 fundamentals to advanced university courses, our platform is designed to be accessible, affordable, and inclusive.

We serve students across the world with the same commitment to quality. Whether you're preparing for an AP exam, mastering algebra, or learning at your own pace through our premium quiz library, Tklesson is built for you.

Fiercely Independent

Tklesson has no corporate parent, no affiliate network, and no third-party integrations that compromise your privacy. We are a standalone platform, built from the ground up, accountable only to the people we serve.

This independence lets us make decisions that are right for our users — not for shareholders, not for partners, not for anyone else. When we say we put you first, we mean it.

These values are not marketing. They are the foundation of every line of code we write,
every policy we set, and every interaction we have with you.

Thank you for trusting Tklesson.
