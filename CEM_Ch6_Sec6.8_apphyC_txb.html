Section 6.8: Energy Integration Calculus

This section demonstrates how calculus can be used to compute energy stored in capacitors and work done in RC circuits.

Energy Stored in a Capacitor:

\[ U = \frac{1}{2} \int_0^Q V \, dq = \frac{1}{2} \frac{Q^2}{C} = \frac{1}{2} C V^2 \]

Where \( V = q/C \) and \( Q \) is the final charge.
