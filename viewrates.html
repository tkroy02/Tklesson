Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book my free first session?

Simply click on any of the "Book Session" buttons on this page to schedule your free 30-minute consultation. This session helps us understand your needs and create a personalized learning plan.

What is your cancellation policy?

We require at least 24 hours’ notice for cancellations. Sessions cancelled with less than 24 hours’ notice will incur the full session fee. Emergency situations are considered on a case-by-case basis.

How long are the tutoring sessions?

Standard sessions are 60 minutes. Extended sessions of 120 minutes are available for students preparing for exams or needing more comprehensive help.

Do you offer refunds for package sessions?

We offer prorated refunds for unused package sessions minus a 10% administrative fee. All refund requests must be made in writing via email.
