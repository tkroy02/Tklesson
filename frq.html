❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do sessions work?

+

Once a session is booked, you'll receive a confirmation email with a meeting link. At the scheduled time, we’ll meet virtually for a one-on-one, interactive lesson tailored to your needs. I use screen sharing, whiteboard tools, and step-by-step guidance to ensure concepts are clear and engaging.

What platforms do you use for lessons?

+

I primarily use Google Meet for all sessions. Links are automatically sent upon booking. No need to install anything if you’re using a browser.

What’s your cancellation/reschedule policy?

+

You may cancel or reschedule up to 24 hours before your session at no charge. Cancellations made less than 24 hours before will incur a 50% fee of the session cost.

Email info@tklesson.com or text +1 226 975 7070 (or +1 951 905 1021) to reschedule.

How long is each session?

+

Most sessions are 60 minutes. Longer sessions can be arranged if needed—just let me know in advance.

How do I book a session?

+

Click the "Book a Session" button on the website. You’ll be directed to my online calendar to choose a time that works best for you.

Do you provide materials or resources?

+

Absolutely. I often share custom worksheets, practice problems, and study guides—many of which I’ve created myself. Students also receive notes or summaries after sessions when needed.

Can I request a trial session?

+

Yes, I offer first-time trial sessions for free so you can experience the tutoring style before committing long-term.

How do I pay for sessions?

+

Payment for sessions should be made before a session. You can click the Payment link on the schedule page (or in your email) to make payment.

Need to Cancel or Reschedule?
