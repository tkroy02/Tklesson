Refund Policy

Our Commitment

At Tklesson, we value your time and investment. Our refund policy is designed to be clear and fair to both clients and instructor.

Refund Terms

  • Full Refund: A full refund (or rescheduling option) will be issued for any session cancelled at least 24 hours before the scheduled time.
  • No Refund: No refund will be given for cancellations made less than 24 hours before the session or for no-shows.
  • Instructor Cancellation: If Tklesson must cancel a session, the client will receive a full refund and will also be entitled to a free session at another time.
  • Processing: Refunds will be processed to the original payment method within 5–10 business days, unless otherwise agreed.
