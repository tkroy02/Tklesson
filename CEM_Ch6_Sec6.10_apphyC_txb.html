Section 6.10: Practice Problems

Test your understanding of advanced RC circuits and multiple loop analysis with these problems.

  1. Analyze a three-loop RC circuit and calculate the currents in each branch.
  2. Determine the voltage across each capacitor as a function of time for a two-loop network.
  3. Find the time constant of a series-parallel RC combination.
  4. Explain the transient response of multiple loop RC circuits.
  5. Compute total energy stored in the capacitors and energy dissipated in resistors over time.
