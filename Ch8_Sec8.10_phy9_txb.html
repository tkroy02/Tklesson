Section 8.10: Chapter 8 Test

This test focuses on wave calculations and definitions. Attempt all questions carefully.

Part A: Calculations

  1. A wave has a frequency of 10 Hz and a wavelength of 2 m. Find its speed.
  2. The period of a wave is 0.05 s. Calculate its frequency.
  3. A sound wave has a speed of 340 m/s and wavelength 0.5 m. Find the frequency.
  4. A car moves toward a stationary horn at 20 m/s. Horn frequency is 500 Hz, speed of sound is 340 m/s. Find observed frequency.
  5. A person moves away from a stationary horn at 10 m/s. Horn frequency 400 Hz, speed of sound 340 m/s. Find observed frequency.
  6. A wave travels 100 m in 5 s. Calculate its speed.
  7. A wave has a speed of 3 m/s and frequency 6 Hz. Find the wavelength.
  8. The period of a wave is 0.02 s and speed is 340 m/s. Find wavelength.
  9. A train horn emits 600 Hz while moving toward a stationary observer at 30 m/s. Speed of sound is 340 m/s. Find the observed frequency.
  10. A transverse wave on a string has wavelength 0.2 m and period 0.01 s. Find its speed.
