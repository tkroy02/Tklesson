Our Courses

Empower your learning journey with our comprehensive high school and AP courses.

Physics

AP Physics 1

An algebra-based course covering Newtonian mechanics, work, energy, power, mechanical waves, and sound.

AP Physics 2

An algebra-based course covering fluid mechanics, thermodynamics, electricity, magnetism, optics, and quantum physics.

AP Physics C (Mechanics)

A calculus-based course focusing on classical mechanics, including kinematics, dynamics, and rotational motion.

AP Physics C (E&M)

A calculus-based course on electricity and magnetism, exploring electrostatics, circuits, and magnetic fields.

Physics Grade 9 & 10

Foundational physics concepts for high school students, building a strong base for future studies.

Physics Grade 11

Intermediate-level physics covering dynamics, energy transformations, and waves.

Physics Grade 12

Advanced topics in physics, including momentum, fields, and modern physics concepts.

Mathematics

Algebra 1

Introduction to algebraic expressions, linear equations, functions, and inequalities.

Algebra 2

Covers advanced functions, complex numbers, polynomials, and conic sections.

Geometry

A comprehensive course on Euclidean geometry, including proofs, congruence, and similarity.

Pre-Calculus

Preparation for calculus, covering trigonometry, sequences, series, and analytic geometry.

AP Calculus

A rigorous introduction to calculus, including limits, derivatives, integrals, and their applications.

Chemistry

Chemistry Grade 10

An introductory course to the principles of chemistry and chemical reactions.

Chemistry Grade 11

Intermediate chemistry focusing on stoichiometry, gas laws, and chemical bonding.

Chemistry Grade 12

Advanced topics including equilibrium, kinetics, and electrochemistry.

Biology

Biology Grade 9

Explores fundamental concepts of life science, from cells to ecosystems.

Biology Grade 10

Continues the exploration of biology with an emphasis on genetics and evolution.

Biology Grade 11

In-depth study of human anatomy, physiology, and plant biology.

Biology Grade 12

Advanced biology topics, including molecular genetics and biodiversity.

AP Biology

College-level biology with a focus on evolution, cellular processes, heredity, and ecology.

Economics

Economics

An introduction to economic principles, including supply and demand, markets, and economic systems.

AP Macroeconomics

A college-level course covering aggregate economic phenomena like inflation, unemployment, and economic growth.

AP Microeconomics

A college-level course focused on the study of individual economic agents and their decision-making.
