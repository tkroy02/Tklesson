Video Interview Guidelines

Your final step to becoming a Tklesson tutor

Welcome to the final stage of your Tklesson tutor application!

This page contains all the information you need to create and submit your teaching demonstration video.

Your Assignment

Create a 5-minute teaching demonstration video covering these three points:

1
1 Minute

Why Tklesson?
Share your passion for tutoring and why you're a good fit for our platform

2
1 Minute

Teaching Beginners
Describe your approach to teaching students with very little subject knowledge

3
3 Minutes

Concept Explanation
Explain a simple concept in your subject as if teaching a beginner student

Total Time: 5 minutes

Step-by-Step Instructions

  • Record your video covering the three points above (5 minutes total)
  • Upload to Google Drive (create a free account if needed)
  • Set sharing permissions to "Anyone with the link can view"
  • Copy the shareable link from Google Drive
  • Reply to our email (info@tklesson.com) with your video link

Technical Requirements

Your video must meet these basic quality standards:

Face clearly visible
Clear audio quality
Good lighting on face
One continuous take preferred
Choose your own concept
Maximum 5 minutes

Pro Tips for Success

Be authentic: We want to see your natural teaching style, not a rehearsed performance.

Part 3 is most important: The concept explanation (3 minutes) shows us your actual teaching ability.

Choose wisely: Pick a concept you're passionate about and can explain simply.

Practice timing: 5 minutes goes quickly! Time yourself before recording.

How You'll Be Evaluated

We assess three key areas of teaching ability:

Communication

40% of score

Clarity, pacing, engagement, and enthusiasm

Teaching Ability

40% of score

Logical explanation, examples, checking for understanding

Professionalism

20% of score

Presentation, preparation, and teaching philosophy

Submission Deadline

5 Days from Receipt of Email

Late submissions cannot be considered due to high application volume.
Please submit your video link via email reply within the deadline.

We look forward to seeing your teaching demonstration!
