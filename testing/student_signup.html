Email Verification Required

After signing up, you'll receive a verification email. Please check:

  • Your inbox for our verification email

  • That you entered your email correctly

Note: You must verify your email before you can log in.

Permission Issues?

If you see "Missing or insufficient permissions" after signing up, this is likely due to Firestore security rules.

Please contact the administrator to ensure your user account has the proper permissions.
