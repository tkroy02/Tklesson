Section 9.8: Mixed Practice

Practice a combination of all rational expressions topics: simplify, multiply/divide, add/subtract, complex fractions, solve equations, and real-life applications.

Mixed Problems

  1. Simplify: \(\dfrac{x^2 - 4}{x^2 - 2x}\)
  2. Multiply: \(\dfrac{2x}{3} \cdot \dfrac{9}{4x}\)
  3. Add: \(\dfrac{1}{x} + \dfrac{2}{x+1}\)
  4. Complex fraction: \(\dfrac{\tfrac{1}{a}+\tfrac{1}{b}}{\tfrac{1}{a}}\)
  5. Solve equation: \(\dfrac{3}{x} - \dfrac{2}{x+1} = 1\)
  6. Word problem: A faucet fills a tank in 6 hours, another in 4 hours. How long together?
  7. Simplify: \(\dfrac{y^2 - 9}{y^2 - 6y + 9}\)
  8. Divide: \(\dfrac{x^2 - 16}{x+4} \div \dfrac{x-4}{x}\)
  9. Solve: \(\dfrac{x}{x+2} + \dfrac{2}{x} = 3\)
  10. Application: A car travels 100 miles downstream in 2 hours, back upstream in 5 hours. Find boat and current speed.
