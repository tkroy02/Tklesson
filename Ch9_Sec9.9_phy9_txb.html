Section 9.9: Chapter 9 Review Questions

These review questions cover key topics from Chapter 9: The Nature of Light, Electromagnetic Spectrum, Reflection, Mirrors, and Lenses.

Questions

  1. Define light as a form of energy.
  2. State two properties of light waves.
  3. List the regions of the electromagnetic spectrum in order of increasing wavelength.
  4. What is the speed of light in a vacuum?
  5. Explain the difference between specular and diffuse reflection.
  6. State the laws of reflection.
  7. Define the focal length of a concave and convex mirror.
  8. An object 15 cm from a concave mirror with focal length 10 cm. Find the image distance using the mirror equation.
  9. A convex mirror has a focal length of 20 cm. An object is placed 30 cm from the mirror. Find the image distance and state whether the image is real or virtual.
  10. Define magnification and give its formula.
  11. Compare the characteristics of images formed by concave and convex mirrors.
  12. Explain why the image in a plane mirror is always virtual and upright.
  13. A concave mirror forms an image twice the size of an object placed 15 cm from the mirror. Determine the image distance and focal length.
  14. State the difference between converging and diverging lenses.
  15. Draw a ray diagram for a convex lens showing a real image.
  16. Draw a ray diagram for a concave lens showing a virtual image.
  17. What is the relation between object distance (\(u\)), image distance (\(v\)), and focal length (\(f\)) for lenses?
  18. Explain why the image formed by a convex mirror is always diminished.
  19. Give two applications of concave mirrors in daily life.
  20. Give two applications of convex mirrors in daily life.
