2.9 Review Questions

Answer the following questions to test your understanding of SI units, derived units, conversions, and dimensional analysis.

Conceptual Questions

  1. Explain why SI units are important in scientific measurements.
  2. Differentiate between base units and derived units with examples.
  3. Why is dimensional analysis useful when checking physics equations?
  4. List three common non-SI units still used in everyday measurements.
  5. Discuss the difference between accuracy and precision in measurements.

Conversion Practice

  1. Convert 12 days to hours.
  2. Convert 95,360 seconds to hours.
  3. Convert 3 months to hours (assume 1 month = 30 days).
  4. Convert 60 km/h to m/s.
  5. Convert 3 m² to mm².
  6. Convert 12 cm² to mm².
