Section 6.9: Conceptual Questions

This section provides conceptual questions to test your understanding of oscillatory motion, energy, damping, and resonance.

Conceptual Questions

  1. Explain why the total energy in SHM remains constant for an ideal mass-spring system.
  2. Describe the effect of damping on amplitude and frequency of oscillation.
  3. How does the amplitude of a driven oscillator depend on the driving frequency?
  4. What is the difference between underdamped, critically damped, and overdamped motion?
  5. Why does resonance occur when the driving frequency equals the natural frequency?
  6. How is energy transferred in a pendulum from potential to kinetic and back?
  7. Discuss how the phase difference between force and displacement affects work in a driven oscillator.
  8. What factors determine the period of a simple pendulum for small oscillations?
  9. Explain the physical significance of the damping ratio in mechanical oscillators.
  10. How does the stiffness of the spring affect the frequency of SHM?
