Physics Schemes of Work (Grades 9–12)

Grade 9 Physics

Semester 1

  • Weeks 1–2: Introduction & Scientific Method
  • Weeks 3–4: Measurement & Units
  • Weeks 5–6: Motion & Graphs
  • Weeks 7–8: Forces & Newton's Laws
  • Weeks 9–10: Work, Energy, and Power

Semester 2

  • Weeks 1–2: Heat & Temperature
  • Weeks 3–4: Waves & Sound
  • Weeks 5–6: Light & Reflection
  • Weeks 7–8: Electricity Basics
  • Weeks 9–10: Review & Projects

Grade 10 Physics

Semester 1

  • Weeks 1–2: Kinematics (1D & 2D)

  • Weeks 5–6: Work, Energy, Conservation
  • Weeks 7–8: Momentum & Collisions
  • Weeks 9–10: Circular Motion

Semester 2

  • Weeks 1–2: Waves & Simple Harmonic Motion
  • Weeks 3–4: Sound & Acoustics
  • Weeks 5–6: Light & Optics
  • Weeks 7–8: Electricity & Circuits
  • Weeks 9–10: Magnetism

Grade 11 Physics

Semester 1

  • Weeks 1–2: Advanced Kinematics
  • Weeks 3–4: Newton's Laws Applications
  • Weeks 5–6: Energy & Power
  • Weeks 7–8: Momentum & Impulse
  • Weeks 9–10: Rotational Motion

Semester 2

  • Weeks 1–2: Fluid Mechanics
  • Weeks 3–4: Thermodynamics
  • Weeks 5–6: Oscillations & Waves
  • Weeks 7–8: Electricity & Magnetism
  • Weeks 9–10: Modern Physics Intro

Grade 12 Physics

Semester 1

  • Weeks 1–2: Advanced Mechanics
  • Weeks 3–4: Gravitation & Satellites
  • Weeks 5–6: Oscillations & Resonance
  • Weeks 7–8: Electric Fields & Potential
  • Weeks 9–10: Magnetic Fields & Induction

Semester 2

  • Weeks 1–2: Alternating Current & Circuits
  • Weeks 3–4: Quantum Physics
  • Weeks 5–6: Atomic & Nuclear Physics

  • Weeks 9–10: Review & Capstone Project
