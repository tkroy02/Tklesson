Section 6.10: Chapter 6 Test

Answer the following problems to test your understanding of AC circuits and related concepts:

  1. Compute the RMS current in a series RLC circuit with R = 25 Ω, L = 0.15 H, C = 60 μF, supplied by 120 V, 60 Hz AC.
  2. Determine the resonance frequency of a series RLC circuit with L = 0.25 H and C = 100 μF.
  3. A transformer has a primary voltage of 240 V and a turns ratio of 5:1. Calculate the secondary voltage.
  4. Find the impedance of a series RC circuit where R = 40 Ω and C = 80 μF at 50 Hz.
  5. Calculate the power factor of a series RL circuit with R = 50 Ω, L = 0.2 H, and an applied voltage of 220 V, 50 Hz.
  6. Determine the average power consumed in a resistor of 20 Ω with RMS current of 3 A.
  7. Calculate the total reactance of a parallel LC circuit with L = 0.1 H and C = 50 μF at 60 Hz.
  8. Find the phase angle in a series RLC circuit where R = 30 Ω, X_L = 25 Ω, X_C = 15 Ω.
  9. Compute the RMS voltage if a peak voltage of 170 V is applied across a series RLC circuit with a peak current of 2 A.
  10. A series RLC circuit has R = 35 Ω, L = 0.1 H, C = 60 μF. Calculate the resonance angular frequency and the total impedance at resonance.
