Section 5.10: Practice Problems

Test your understanding of basic electric circuits including resistors, Ohm’s Law, power, and RC circuits.

Problems

  1. A 12 V battery is connected to a series circuit with resistors 4 Ω and 6 Ω. Find the current and voltage across each resistor.
  2. Calculate the equivalent resistance of three resistors in parallel: 2 Ω, 3 Ω, and 6 Ω.
  3. A 10 µF capacitor charges through a 1 kΩ resistor. Find the time constant and voltage at t = 2 ms if the battery is 5 V.
  4. Explain why series and parallel circuits behave differently when adding resistors.
  5. Draw the charging and discharging curves for an RC circuit with τ = 0.01 s.
  6. Compute the power dissipated in a resistor of 10 Ω with a current of 2 A flowing through it.
  7. Compare the energy stored in capacitors of 5 µF and 10 µF connected to the same 12 V source.
  8. Derive the formula for current in an RC series circuit during charging.
  9. For a series RC circuit, after how many time constants will the capacitor be charged to over 95% of the supply voltage?
  10. Explain the physical meaning of the RC time constant in simple words.
