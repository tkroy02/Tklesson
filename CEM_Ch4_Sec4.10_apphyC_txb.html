Section 4.10: Practice Problems

Test your understanding of conductors, capacitors, and dielectrics with the following problems.

Practice Problems

  1. Compute the capacitance of a parallel-plate capacitor with a uniform dielectric of permittivity \( \epsilon_r \) and plate separation \( d \).
  2. A capacitor has two dielectrics in series. Determine the effective capacitance.
  3. Find the energy stored in a capacitor with nonuniform electric field given \( E(x) = E_0(1 + x/d) \).
  4. Explain the distribution of surface charges on a conductor in electrostatic equilibrium.
  5. Calculate the voltage across a capacitor when a dielectric slab partially fills it.
